FERRERO ROCHER AND RAFFAELLO

Ferrero reached and closed out a number of its 2020 targets having made substantial progress, for instance it has achieved its goal of sourcing 100% sustainable cocoa beans through independently managed schemes and 100% certified cane sugar through Bonsucro and Altromercato.

Despite the challenges presented by the global pandemic, Ferrero adapted successfully, putting the health and safety of its employees and consumers at the forefront of its efforts. A critical focus has been to protect workers and farmers in its agricultural supply chains, especially in less developed markets.
